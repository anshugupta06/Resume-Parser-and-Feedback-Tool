How to Create a Resume

  • Choose the right resume format.
  • Include your name and contact information.
  • Add a resume summary or objective.
  • List your soft and hard skills.
  • Include your work experience and certifications.

Watch this detailed video: How to create a resume.

How to Improve Your ATS Score

  • Use job-specific keywords.
  • Keep formatting simple.
  • Customize your resume for each job.
  • Avoid graphics or tables that ATS can't read.
  • Test your resume with online ATS tools.

Watch this helpful video: How to improve ATS score.

How to Focus on Skills

  • Identify your most relevant skills.
  • Only include job-relevant skills.
  • Separate soft and hard skills.
  • Indicate proficiency levels for hard skills.
  • Showcase skills through work experience.

Learn more here: How to focus on skills.

How to Claim the Best Certifications

  • Explore certifications from top companies like Amazon, Google, and Microsoft.
  • Choose certifications relevant to your field.
  • Check for free or discounted courses.
  • Prepare for and pass certification exams.
  • Showcase certifications on your resume and LinkedIn profile.
